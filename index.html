FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does installation take?

Most residential projects take 7-14 days from measurement to installation. For big projects like plazas, we give you a clear timeline after quotation.

Do you give warranty?

Yes 100%. We offer 2 years warranty on all fabrication and installation work. If anything happens, we come and fix it.

Do you serve areas outside Ojo?

Yes. We cover all of Lagos including Ijanikin, Alaba, Iba, Okokomaiko, Lagos Island, Victoria Island and Lekki.

What type of materials do you use?

We use only premium grade aluminium profiles and 5mm to 10mm toughened glass. No cutting corners. Quality that lasts 15+ years.
