FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1. How long does installation take?

Most residential projects take 7-14 days from measurement to final installation. Big commercial projects may take longer depending on size.

2. Do you work outside Ojo?

Yes. We cover all of Lagos including Ijanikin, Alaba, Iba, Ajah, Lekki, VI, Ikeja and other areas.

3. What type of glass do you use?

We use 5mm to 12mm toughened glass and premium aluminium profiles. All materials are rust-proof and weather resistant.

4. Can you design custom kitchen cupboards?

Yes 100%. We measure your kitchen space and design custom aluminium cupboards that fit perfectly and last long.

5. How do I get a quote?

Just click the WhatsApp button or fill the contact form. Send us pictures of your space and we will send you a free quote within 24 hours.
